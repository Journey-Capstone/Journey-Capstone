A Message From Our Creators

Reading has always been a passion of ours. Not just to improve brain power, but also to exercise our imagination. We see new books as new journeys, as we allow our imaginations to take us there. And sometimes, it's nice to re-take a journey that we once enjoyed, so some of us choose to read a book a second and third time.

We get together, and we talk about our journeys, and that sparks the interests of others'. So we have set out to build a website that gives us an opportunity to view other users' bookshelves, and view their perspectives of their journey.

So here we are. On this Journey. Where you can view the booklists of like-minded readers, or those that are not like-minded; and vice-versa. Where you can write the reviews, and create your booklist. Where you can find your next favorite book, and go onward to your next Journey.
